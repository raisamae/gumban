The Patient-Centered Method

The needs, preferences, and values of each patient are given top priority in all facets of their care when using a patient-centered approach in healthcare. In order to empower patients and enhance their general well-being, it places a strong emphasis on respect, cooperation, and shared decision-making between the patient and their healthcare team. .
